The
Artists

NOVO Quartet

Founded in 2018, NOVO Quartet plays 100+ concerts a year across world-class venues and remote outreach destinations alike. BBC New Generation Artists 2025–27, winners at Geneva, Heidelberg, Carl Nielsen & Trondheim — their debut album earned 5 stars in Politiken and Editor's Choice in Gramophone.

Full Bio → www.novoquartet.com

Paddington Trio

Critically acclaimed as "three virtuosic musicians who play as one" (The Guardian), Tuulia Hero, Patrick Moriarty, and Stephanie Tang met at London's Paddington Station. First Prize at the Royal Over-Seas League Competition and Parkhouse Award. Their debut album 'Freakshow' arrives on PENTATONE in 2027.

Full Bio → www.paddingtontrio.com

Simply Quartet

Four first prizes at major competitions including Carl Nielsen and Quatuor à Bordeaux. ECHO Rising Stars alumni, Simply Quartet are regulars at Wigmore Hall, the Elbphilharmonie, and Concertgebouw Amsterdam. Their debut album on Genuin (2025) was hailed as "breathtakingly precise" by SWR Kultur.

Full Bio → www.simplyquartet.com
Anders Malta Trumpet Joins on Sunday, Sep 13 at 14:00 for an afternoon of jazz with colleagues, and as the composer of Affaldssortering / Waste Sorting.

About the Festival

A festival for
all curious
listeners

A classical music festival set in the charming LiteraturHaus in Nørrebro. The bar stays open, the atmosphere is informal, and the musicians are right there in front of you — whether you're new to classical music or have loved it for years.
